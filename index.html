THE DEFFENSIVE ATTACK OF BANJAR PEOPLE 1868

 

The frontal attack between the banjar military troops and the Dutch East Indies Colonial armies, which ended with the victory for the Dutch.

THE BATTLE OF KUSAMBA 1849

 

The paramilitary troops of Kusamba under the leadership of I Dewa Agung Putra Kusamba were attacking the dutch Army bunker under the leadership of General Michiels. In the battle General Michiels was killed.

THE BATTLE OF JAGARAGA 1848-1849

 

The defensive counter attack of Balinese people under the leadership of Minister Jelantik against the invading Dutch East Indies Colonial Army in front of Jagaraga Fort.

THE PUPUTAN BADUNG EVENT 1906

 

King of Badung with all of his relatives and his people, all in white, were launching a defensive counter attack, determined to fight to the death againts the Dutch rather than surrender. This Kind of fighting soirit is known as the spirit of

“Puputan”.
About Monumen Bajra Sandhi merupakan Monumen Perjuangan Rakyat Bali untuk memberi hormat pada para pahlawan serta merupakan lambang pesemaian pelestarian jiwa perjuangan rakyat Bali dari generasi ke generasi dan dari zaman ke zaman serta lambang semangat untuk mempertahankan keutuhan Negara Kesatuan Republik Indonesia. Hal ini dapat dilihat dari 17 anak tangga yang ada di pintu utama, 8 buah tiang agung di dalam gedung monumen, dan monumen yang menjulang setinggi 45 meter.
